Various lenses available

There are several types of eye lenses that are used to correct vision problems or to improve visual function, we have the;

  • Single vision lenses, these lenses have a single prescription power that is used to correct either nearsightedness or farsightedness.
    • Images of single vision lenses


  • Bifocal lenses as the same states has two different prescription powers in the same lens. The upper part of the lens is used for distance vision, and the lower part is used for near vision.
    • Images of bifocal lenses


  • Trifocal lenses is another we have available,they have three different prescription powers in the same lens. The upper part of the lens is used for distance vision, the middle part is
    used for intermediate vision, and the lower part is used for near vision.
    • Images of trifocal lenses.


  • Progressive lenses have a gradual change in prescription power from the top of the lens to the bottom, which allows for a more natural transition between distance, intermediate, and near vision.
    • Images of Progressive lenses
