Our Services

Here at Lukeman we offer a lot of services that concerns the eye.

  • We provide comprehensive eye examination,these exams assess the overall health of your eyes, including vision acuity,
    eye movements, eye pressure, and eye structure.
  • We also provide Contact lens fitting, which involve measuring your eyes and prescribing the correct type of contact lens for your needs.
  • We offer treatment for eye diseases. Here at Lukeman we provide diagnosis and treatment of a range of eye conditions, such as glaucoma, cataracts, macular degeneration, and diabetic retinopathy. Our ophthalmologists are available to treat any illness concerning the eye.
  • We also offer Pediatric eye care, a higher percentage of children suffer from an eye problem so we are here to provide specialized care for children's eye health, including screening for amblyopia (lazy eye), strabismus (crossed eyes), and other vision problems they may have.You can when you see any problem developing in your child's eyes.
  • As the name states we offer Optical services too, we have optical shops that offer a range of eyeglasses, sunglasses, and other vision aids.There are you can choose from, depending the problem you have.
